Fajita Tacos

Description

Who doesn't fucking love tacos?

Because why the fuck not?


Ingredients

  • Go to your local meat market for some marinated beef fajita. Get however much you think you will need. You are a smart bitch.
  • Tomatoes whatever kind and however much you want.
  • Some cilantro
  • Red onion
  • Corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Grill the meat until it is done.
  2. Cut up the tomatoes, cilantro, and onion and combine in a large bowl.
  3. Toast the corn tortillas.
  4. Serve the tacos. Add your favorite hot sauce.
