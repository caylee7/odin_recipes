Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

Description

A simple spaghetti recipe that is perfect for any weeknight.

This classic recipe has been in our family for years. This tastes like home. There is a secret ingredient that takes out the acidity!


Ingredients

  • 1 pack spaghetti or angel hair noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 pack ground italian sausage
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  • 1 pinch baking soda

Steps

  1. Boil about 2 quarts of water and add the noodles to it, following the directions on the package for the amount of time.
  2. Form the italian sausage into meatballs. Brown and cook the meatballs in a pan. Once done, set aside.
  3. In the same pan, cook the ground beef. Add salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste.
  4. Add the marinara sauce to the pan. Cook on medium heat until warm. Season to taste.
  5. Add back in the meatballs to the sauce.
  6. Add some pasta water to the sauce. Who knows how much or what it even does... Serve with crusty bread!
  7. Almost forgot the baking soda...Add that in with the sauce. Don't add too much or it will taste kind of weird.
  8. Serve it. It's spaghetti. You know how to serve it.
